Welcome to Offprint!

Hey there!

Say hello to Offprint—a website built from the ground up to foster an engaging, friendly, and open community of writers and readers.

The site is currently under heavy active development, so there's a lot of stuff still missing or even completely broken. Want to sign up? Click here to add your name to the list!

If you want to contribute—or just want to give us a star—check out our GitHub repository. We also have a community Discord server if you want to chat about the site, or writing in general. And if you want to throw some money our way, to help fund Offprint's development, we have a Patreon, as well as a Ko-Fi for one-time donations! If you already have an account, please use this support page for any issues you'd like to report.

With all that said, see you guys on the flip side!
